Procedures & Approach

Ovarian Cancer Surgery

Staging laparotomy, cytoreductive surgery, and interval debulking for ovarian and fallopian tube malignancies.

Endometrial (Uterine) Cancer Surgery

Total hysterectomy with bilateral salpingo-oophorectomy and nodal assessment; laparoscopic approach where suitable.

Cervical Cancer Surgery

Radical hysterectomy with pelvic lymph node dissection for early-stage cervical carcinoma.

Vulvar Cancer Surgery

Wide local excision or radical vulvectomy with inguinal lymph node dissection.

When to Seek Consultation

  • Abnormal vaginal bleeding or pelvic pain
  • Adnexal mass or raised tumour markers (CA-125, HE4)
  • Newly diagnosed gynaecologic malignancy
  • Planning for fertility-sparing surgery
