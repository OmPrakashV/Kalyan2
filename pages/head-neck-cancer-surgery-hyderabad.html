Head & Neck Oncology

Head & Neck Cancer Surgery in Hyderabad

Dr. Kalyan Vangara performs surgical management of head and neck malignancies including oral, thyroid, laryngeal, and salivary gland cancers at Amor Hospitals, Kukatpally, Hyderabad.

Procedures & Approach

Oral & Oropharyngeal Cancer Surgery

Wide local excision, hemimandibulectomy, and reconstruction for oral cavity and oropharyngeal malignancies.

Thyroid Cancer Surgery

Total thyroidectomy and central or lateral neck dissection for papillary, follicular, medullary, and anaplastic thyroid cancers.

Neck Dissection

Selective, modified radical, or radical neck dissection to address cervical lymph node metastases.

Salivary Gland Tumours

Parotidectomy and submandibular gland excision for benign and malignant salivary tumours.

When to Seek Consultation

  • A persistent neck lump or swelling
  • Hoarseness, difficulty swallowing, or a non-healing mouth ulcer
  • Thyroid nodule or abnormal thyroid scan
  • Newly diagnosed head or neck malignancy
