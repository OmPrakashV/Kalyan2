Thoracic & Orthopaedic Oncology

Thoracic & Orthopaedic Oncology Surgery in Hyderabad

Dr. Kalyan Vangara provides surgical management for thoracic and musculoskeletal tumours, including lung cancers, soft tissue sarcomas, and bone tumours, at Amor Hospitals, Hyderabad.

Procedures & Approach

Lung Cancer

Surgical resection (lobectomy, segmentectomy) for primary lung tumours; thoracoscopic approaches where feasible.

Soft Tissue Sarcoma

Wide local excision and compartmental resection of soft tissue sarcomas in limbs and trunk.

Bone Tumours

Limb-sparing surgery for primary bone tumours; curettage, bone grafting, and prosthetic reconstruction.

Rib & Chest Wall Tumours

En-bloc resection of rib and chest wall tumours with reconstruction.
