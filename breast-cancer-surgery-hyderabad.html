Procedures & Approach

Breast Conservation Surgery (BCS)

Removing the tumour with a clear margin while preserving the breast where possible, often combined with radiation therapy.

Oncoplastic Reconstruction

Combining oncologic surgery with plastic surgery techniques to maintain breast shape and appearance after tumour removal.

Modified Radical Mastectomy

Removal of the breast along with axillary lymph nodes; recommended for advanced or multifocal disease.

Axillary Lymph Node Dissection / Sentinel Node Biopsy

Staging procedure to assess lymph node involvement and guide adjuvant treatment decisions.
